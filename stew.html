Rice and Stew Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cups water/chicken broth
  • 2 teaspoons thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

Method of Preparation

  1. Rinse the rice and cook it in water or chicken broth until tender (about 20 minutes).
  2. In a separate pot, heat oil and saut√© the chopped onions.
  3. Add tomato paste, thyme, salt, and pepper; cook for about 10 minutes until thick.
  4. Combine cooked rice with the stew and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, and enjoy with your choice of protein!
