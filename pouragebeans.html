Porridge Beans Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried beans
  • 3-4 cups water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Seasoning cubes (optional)
  • 1-2 tablespoons palm oil (optional)

Method of Preparation

  1. Soak the beans overnight or boil for about 10-15 minutes to soften.
  2. In a pot, combine soaked beans with water, onion, and boil until cooked and tender.
  3. Stir in salt and palm oil, if using, and cook for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  4. Serve hot, optionally with bread or plantains.
