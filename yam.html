Yam Porridge Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of yam, peeled and diced
  • 3 cups of water
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 tablespoons palm oil
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetables (e.g., spinach or ugu leaves)

Method of Preparation

  1. In a pot, bring water to boil and add the diced yam.
  2. Cook until the yam is tender, then mash slightly with a wooden spoon.
  3. Add chopped onion, palm oil, seasoning cubes, and salt, stirring well.
  4. Stir in vegetables and let it cook for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your yam porridge!
