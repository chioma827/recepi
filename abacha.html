Abacha Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of dried abacha (African salad)
  • 1 cup of palm oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 tablespoons ground crayfish
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh pepper (to taste)
  • Vegetables (e.g. ugwu or any leafy green)

Method of Preparation

  1. Soak the abacha in cold water for about 30 minutes until soft, then drain.
  2. In a pot, heat palm oil on low heat, add chopped onion, and stir until softened.
  3. Mix in the ground crayfish, salt, and pepper, cooking for about 5 minutes.
  4. Add soaked abacha, mix well, and cook for another 5-10 minutes.
  5. Serve warm or cold, topped with vegetables if desired.
