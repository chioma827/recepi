Beans Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried beans (Black-eyed peas, Pinto beans, etc.)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cups of water
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: spices or seasoning cubes

Method of Preparation

  1. Soak the beans in water overnight or for a few hours to soften.
  2. In a pot, combine soaked beans, chopped onion, and water. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer until the beans are tender (about 1-2 hours).
  4. Add salt and any additional seasonings, stirring well.
  5. Serve hot, optionally with rice or bread.
