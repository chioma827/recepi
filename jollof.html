Jollof Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes (or 4 fresh tomatoes)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method of Preparation

  1. In a pot, fry the chopped onions in some oil until translucent.
  2. Add the tomato paste and diced tomatoes, cooking for about 10 minutes.
  3. Add the chicken broth, thyme, bay leaves, and season with salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Add the rice and chopped bell pepper to the pot, stirring to combine.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.
  6. Fluff with a fork and serve hot.
