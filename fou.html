Fou-Fou and Vegetable Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Fou-Fou flour (cassava or plantain)
  • 4 cups of water (for cooking)
  • 2 cups of mixed vegetables (e.g., spinach, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 1 piece of fish or meat (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Seasoning cubes

Method of Preparation

  1. In a pot, bring water to boil and gradually add the Fou-Fou flour, stirring continuously until it forms a smooth dough.
  2. In a separate pot, boil water and add your vegetables and seasonings.
  3. Add the fish or meat if using, and cook until the meat is tender.
  4. in one put add the cooked vegetables and the fish or meat stock.
  5. add your palm oli. and stir leave the mixture for about 5 miniutes
  6. Serve the Fou-Fou with the hot vegetable soup on the side for dipping.
