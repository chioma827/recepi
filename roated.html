Roasted Plantain Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe plantains
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil (optional for brushing)

Method of Preparation

  1. Preheat a grill or oven to medium-high heat.
  2. Peel the plantains and slice them in half lengthwise or leave them whole.
  3. Optionally, brush with oil and sprinkle with salt.
  4. Place the plantains on the grill or in the oven, cooking for about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Serve hot as a snack or side dish.
