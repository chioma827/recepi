Okpa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Okpa flour
  • 1 onion, blended
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup palm oil
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste

Method of Preparation

  1. In a bowl, mix the Okpa flour, blended onion, palm oil, salt, and seasoning cubes.
  2. Gradually add water to form a batter.
  3. Pour the mixture into banana leaves or small containers, and steam for about 30-45 minutes.
  4. Serve hot as a snack or side dish.
