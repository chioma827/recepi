Plantain Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe plantains
  • Salt to taste
  • Olive oil (optional)

Method of Preparation

  1. Peel the plantains and slice them into discs or lengthwise.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a little oil if desired.
  3. Add the plantain slices and fry until golden brown on both sides (about 3-4 minutes per side).
  4. Remove from heat, sprinkle with salt, and serve warm.
