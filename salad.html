Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens (e.g., lettuce, spinach)
  • 1 cup of diced vegetables (tomato, cucumber, bell pepper)
  • 1/2 cup of diced avocado
  • 1/4 cup of feta cheese (optional)
  • Dressing (olive oil, vinegar, honey)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method of Preparation

  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, diced vegetables, and avocado.
  2. Top with feta cheese if using.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper for dressing.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh salad!
