Fried Rice with Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, diced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the beaten eggs and scramble until fully cooked. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of vegetable oil.
  4. Add the cooked chicken and saut√© for about 2 minutes until heated through.
  5. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for another 2-3 minutes until they are tender.
  6. Stir in the cooked rice, breaking up any clumps. Mix well.
  7. Add the soy sauce and sesame oil (if using), and stir to combine. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Finally, add the scrambled eggs and chopped green onions. Stir well until everything is evenly mixed.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your fried rice with chicken!
