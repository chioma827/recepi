White Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of meat (goat, chicken, or fish)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cups of water
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons ground crayfish
  • 2 tablespoons of palm oil (optional)
  • Fresh pepper (to taste)

Method of Preparation

  1. In a pot, add the meat, chopped onion, water, and seasoning. Boil until tender.
  2. Add ground crayfish and fresh pepper to the pot and stir well.
  3. Optional: Add palm oil for richness. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Serve hot with a side of starch or rice.
